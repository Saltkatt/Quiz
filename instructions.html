Instructions

    This quiz consists of 10 random TRUE or FALSE questions.
    If you answer correctly the button you have pressed will turn green. If your answer is incorrect the button pressed will turn red.
    A progressbar will show how many questions you have answered. Att the end of the quiz your score will be show up att the bottom.
    Once you are done and want to play again refresh the page. If you do not want to play again close or leave the page.
    Click Quiz in the navigation bar above to get to the random quiz.
